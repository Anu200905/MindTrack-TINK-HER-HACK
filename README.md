### Project Description
MindTrack is a mental health tracking app that helps students monitor mood and stress levels. It provides insights and simple support tools to improve emotional well-being.

### The Problem statement
[What problem are you solving?]

### The Solution
Students face stress and emotional pressure but do not have an easy way to track or manage their mental health. A simple system is needed to help them monitor their mood and get timely support.
